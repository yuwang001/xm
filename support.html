Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a new task?
Tap the "+" button on the home screen or any category page. Fill in the task details including title, priority, due date, and optional subtasks or tags, then tap "Add Task".
How do I track a habit?
Go to the Habits tab and tap "+" to create a new habit. Set whether it's daily or weekly, choose which days to track, and customize the color. Tap on a habit to mark it complete for the day.
How does the Focus Timer work?
Navigate to the Focus tab, select a duration (15, 25, 45, or 60 minutes), optionally link it to a task, and tap Start. The timer will run and track your focus session. You'll be notified when the session ends.
How do I export my data?
Go to Settings and find the "Export Data" option. You can export your tasks, habits, and focus sessions in JSON or CSV format, which can be shared or saved for backup.
How do I delete my account?
To delete your account and all associated data, please contact us via email at 646055204@QQ.com with your account email. We will process your request within 48 hours.
Is my data secure?
Yes! Your data is stored securely using Firebase (Google Cloud) with encrypted transmission. We never sell or share your personal information with third parties.
