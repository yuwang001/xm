Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 23, 2025

Welcome to MarkFlow ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

MarkFlow is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal information:

  • Local Data: Your Markdown documents and files are stored locally on your device. We do not upload or access your documents.
  • Purchase Information: If you subscribe to MarkFlow Pro, your purchase is processed through Apple's App Store. We receive only the information necessary to verify your subscription status, but we do not have access to your payment details.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely for:

  • Providing and maintaining the app functionality
  • Verifying subscription status for premium features
  • Improving our services and user experience

3. Data Storage

All your Markdown documents and app data are stored locally on your device. We do not have servers that store your personal files or documents. Your data remains under your control.

4. Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services:

  • Apple App Store: For processing in-app purchases and subscriptions
  • RevenueCat: For subscription management and verification

These services have their own privacy policies governing the use of your information.

5. Subscriptions

MarkFlow offers auto-renewable subscriptions:

  • Monthly subscription with a 3-day free trial
  • Payment will be charged to your iTunes Account after the free trial period
  • Subscription automatically renews unless canceled at least 24 hours before the end of the current period
  • You can manage and cancel your subscriptions by going to your App Store account settings

6. Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

7. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information. Since your documents are stored locally on your device, the security of your data is primarily managed through your device's security features (passcode, Face ID, Touch ID).

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and export your local data at any time
  • Delete the app and all associated local data
  • Cancel your subscription at any time through App Store settings

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
