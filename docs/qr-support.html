Frequently Asked Questions

How do I scan a QR code?

Open the app and tap the "Scan" tab at the bottom. Point your camera at a QR code, and it will be automatically detected and processed.

Can I scan QR codes from saved images?

Yes! On the Scan screen, tap the photo library icon in the top right corner to select an image containing a QR code from your photo library.

How do I create a WiFi QR code?

Go to the "Generate" tab, select "WiFi", enter your network name (SSID), password, and encryption type, then tap "Generate QR Code".

Where is my data stored?

All your data is stored locally on your device. We do not collect or transmit any of your information. Your privacy is fully protected.

How do I delete my scan history?

Go to the "History" tab, tap the menu icon (three dots) in the top right corner, and select "Clear All Records". You can also swipe left on individual items to delete them.

The camera is not working. What should I do?

Make sure you have granted camera permission to the app. Go to your device Settings, find Beautiful QRCode, and enable Camera access. If the issue persists, try restarting the app.

Can I use the app offline?

Yes! Beautiful QRCode works completely offline. You can scan, generate, and manage QR codes without an internet connection.

How do I share a generated QR code?

After generating a QR code, tap the "Save" button to add it to your history. Then open it from the History tab and tap "Share" to send it via messages, email, or other apps.
